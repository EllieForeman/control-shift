    Programme Overview

    We would like to publicly thank everyone who applied to our open call. We were thrilled by the response, and had over 180 applications from all over the world. We wish we could have worked with more artists and hope we will be able to grow to include more works in the future.

    We have selected an exciting mix of brilliant artists and are currently shaping the programme and developing an appropriate format to share it with you. Control Shift will be taking place in the Autumn through online channels and physical safe spaces. Full programme will be announced soon, but you can find out a bit more through our Pervastive Media Studio lunchtime talk.
